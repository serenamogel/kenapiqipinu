Jasmin Bhasin who is one of the prettiest film and TV actress, born in the city of Rajasthan, Kota, India where she grew up and studied. She did her bachelors from a hospitality college located in Jaipur, India. After completing her studies, she then moved into the showbiz line and started appearing in various TV serials. Meanwhile, she marked her identity in the Malayalam, Tamil, Telugu, and Kannada cinema wherein she worked in many movies for which she got acclaimed. Undoubtedly, the young actress is the most appealing and stylish, yet a great actress.

Career

Jasmine Bhasin took the start of her professional career with a debut movie “Vaanam” (Tamil movie) in which she appeared as Priya. While it was released in 2011. In the same year, she appeared in another movie “Karodpati” which was a Kannada movie, and also worked in “Beware of Dogs” (Malayalam movie) in which she appeared as Meghna. This was incredible that she gave three consecutive movies in the same year and holds a special place in the hearts of many. In 2014, she worked in the movie “Dillunodu” and “Veta”. While both these films are in the Telugu language. In 2015, she appeared in another Telugu movie named “Ladies and Gentleman” in which she portrayed the role of Anjali. In 2016, she worked in the movie “Jil Jung Juk” which was a Tamil movie. Afterward, she moved into the small screen and made a debut serial “Tashan-e-Ishq” which became a superhit serial. It was aired on Zee TV from 2015 to 2016 and she portrayed the role of Twinkle Taneja. From 2017 to 2018, she marked an appearance in another famous serial “Dil Se Dil Tak” which was aired on Colors TV. In 2019, Jasmin Bhasin appeared in “Fear Factor: Khatron Ke Khiladi -9”, and also worked in the serial “Dil Toh Happy Hai Ji” which was aired in the same year. From 2019 to 2020, she also appeared in “Naagin- Bhagya Ka Zehreela Khel”. In 2020, she also appeared in the reality TV show named “Fear Factor: Khatron Ke Khiladi” (India). Alongside acting, she has also modeled for many print and TV advertisements.

Achievements

Jasmin Bhasin was honored with the Gold Awards for the category of Debut Female for the debut serial “Tashan-e-Ishq” in the year 2016. Also, she earned a nomination of “Best Jodi” with Sidhant Gupta for the same serial in 2016.
