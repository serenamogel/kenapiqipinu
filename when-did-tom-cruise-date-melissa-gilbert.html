When did Tom Cruise date Melissa Gilbert?

Publish date: 2024-04-18

In the ever-evolving tapestry of Hollywood romances, some relationships stand out, not for their duration, but for the surprise factor they bring. One such fleeting relationship was between two rising stars of the 1980s – Tom Cruise and Melissa Gilbert.

Young Stars in Tinseltown

Before he became an international superstar known for the “Mission: Impossible” series and before she cemented her legacy as Laura Ingalls on “Little House on the Prairie,” Tom and Melissa were just two young actors navigating the maze of Hollywood.

A Whirlwind Romance

The duo dated briefly in the early 1980s. While details of their romance have been mostly private, Melissa Gilbert has occasionally spoken about her time with Cruise in interviews. She described him as a sweet and kind individual, emphasizing the genuine nature of their short-lived relationship.

Their relationship might not have lasted long, but it was certainly passionate. Gilbert, in various interviews, has even mentioned that Cruise was one of her first serious relationships and that she still has fond memories of their time together.

Life After the Relationship

Both Melissa Gilbert and Tom Cruise moved on to other relationships and further solidified their respective careers in Hollywood. Tom Cruise’s romantic endeavors, especially his marriages to Nicole Kidman and Katie Holmes, have frequently been in the spotlight. Meanwhile, Melissa had her fair share of Hollywood romances and marriages, including those with Bo Brinkman, Bruce Boxleitner, and most recently, Timothy Busfield.
