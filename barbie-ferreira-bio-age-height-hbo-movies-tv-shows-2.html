Publish date: 2024-06-02

Barbie Ferreira Biography

Barbara Linhares Ferreira is an American actress and model. Ferreira has earned attention and acclaim for her career and also for being an outspoken supporter of the body positivity movement.

Barbie Ferreira Age

Barbara Linhares Ferreira was born on December 14, 1996 in New York City, U.S.

Barbie Ferreira Early Life

Born in Queens, New York, Ferreira later moved to Maywood, New Jersey and went to Hackensack High School. She was raised by her Brazilian mother and grandmother. Ferreira’s mother works as a chef.

Barbie Ferreira Modeling

Ferreira jumpstarted her career on her own by sending in pictures to American Apparel. Ferreira modeled for the brand while simultaneously working in one of their retail stores. She has since modeled for acclaimed brands like Aerie, Adidas, Asos, H&M, Forever 21, Missguided and Target, among others.

In 2016, unretouched photos and a video interview from Ferriera’s Aerie campaign went viral. Later that year, Time (magazine) named her among their “30 Most Influential Teens.” Other editorial appearances have included Idol, Teen Vogue, Vogue.com, Oyster (Cover), French Grazia (Cover) and Nylon (Cover).

Barbie Ferreira Directing

Barbie Ferreira directed music video “So Cool” by artist Dounia.

Barbie Ferreira Acting

She starred in the 10-episode Vice series “How to Behave,” focused on etiquette. The show explored themes such as sex, money, and body image. The series also earned her recognition from the Webby Awards for “Best Web Personality/Host. Ferreira also starred in a web series for Teen Vogue called “Body Party” where she guided the audience through the various facets of her own mantra of body positivity.

Barbie Ferreira HBO

In addition, she starred together with Sarah Jessica Parker and Thomas Haden Church as “Ella” in two episodes of HBO’s series “Divorce”. Ferreira will continue her relationship with HBO as “Kat” in their upcoming series “Euphoria” starring Zendaya.
