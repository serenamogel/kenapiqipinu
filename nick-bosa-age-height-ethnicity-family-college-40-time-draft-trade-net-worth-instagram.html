College

He committed to play college football at Ohio State University. There, he was named a unanimous First-team All-Big Ten and the Smith-Brown Big Ten Defensive Lineman of the Year.

Draft and Trade (When was he drafted?)

In the first round with the second overall pick in the 2019 NFL Draft, he was selected by the San Francisco 49ers.
