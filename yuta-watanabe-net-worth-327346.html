Japanese-born basketball player who signed with Toronto Raptors during the 2020-21 NBA season. While in college at George Washington University, he won the Atlantic 10 Defensive Player of the Year in 2018.

He comes from a basketball family, as his mother, Kumi, played professionally for the Chanson V-Magic, as well as the Japanese women's national team. His father played professionally for the Kumagai Gumi Bruins, and his sister, Yuki, has played professionally.
