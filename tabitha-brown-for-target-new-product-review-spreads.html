Tabitha Brown for Target Product Review: Spreads

Publish date: 2024-06-11

It’s no surprise that Tabitha Brown has her own line of food and products at Target. She is the original food TikToker. Her charming personality and delicious vegan recipes had everyone obsessed with her from her very first social media post.

Now, you can see her on TV, hear her on podcasts, and read her book. Soon enough, we’ll probably catch her in the White House (just kidding…but maybe not?). We knew we had to try everything we could get our hands on from this limited-release line as soon as it hit shelves. First, the spreads of Tabitha Brown for Target!

New Product!

Vegan Chimichurri Aioli SpreadTarget

I should start by saying this: none of the spreads are bad. They are all, in fact, good! They’re actually exceptional if you’re looking for a vegan alternative to traditional mayo-based products. This chimichurri aioli spread ranked in the middle of the pack. I wish it tasted more like herbs. Chimichurri is super pungent, so I expected a little more from this.

New Product!

Vegan Lemon Garlic Aioli SpreadTarget

This was among our favorite Tabitha Brown for Target spreads. What’s not to love about a creamy aioli with lemon and garlic? Both flavors are bold, but also harmonious. This would be an incredible dipping sauce for fries or a delicious sandwich spread. You could put a dollop of this spread on a grain bowl or use it as a sauce for vegan fish tacos.

Credit: Liv Averett / Target

Rating:

9.5/10

Vegan Truffle Garlic SpreadTarget

To me, this spread is a little bitter in that truffle oil way. I wish it had more of a real truffle flavor. But at this price point, what did I expect? I still think it would be great in a dairy-free grilled cheese or as a dipping sauce for roasted veggies.

Vegan Cilantro Lime Aioli SpreadTarget

I think the lime is a little too tart in this, but only a little. It’s not the most balanced spread, but it’s still tasty. Slap this on a black bean burger with some avocado and you have yourself a delicious and flavorful vegan dinner. I’m certainly not mad at it.

Vegan Spicy Jalapeño Cilantro Garlic SpreadTarget

Sadly, this spread is not spicy. I would’ve loved it if one of the dips in this line had an actual kick. And while I deducted points for the lack of spice, I added tons of points for the tasty flavor! If you like jalapeño, cilantro, garlic, and mayo, you’ll like this.
