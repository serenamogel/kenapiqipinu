Mightyena δ Delta Species
グラエナ δ Graena δ
For more information on this Pokémon's species, see Mightyena.

Mightyena δ (Japanese: グラエナ δ Graena δ) is a dual-type Darkness/Metal Stage 1 Pokémon card. It is part of the EX Delta Species expansion.

Card text

Trivia

It was ruled that, for the purpose of Gang Up, a dual-type Darkness/Metal Pokémon counted only once, meaning that the guaranteed damage for the attack is 10, not the 20 it would have been guaranteed had the two types been counted separately.
