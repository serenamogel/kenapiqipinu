Transfusion 2023 Starring Phoebe Tonkin and Sam Worthington Available For Streaming on Stan

Publish date: 2024-02-17

Transfusion 2023 cast is headed by Sam Worthington and other members include Phoebe Tonkin, Matt Nable, and Edward Carmody. Sam is portraying Ryan Logan in the film.

The film was released on January 20, 2023, in Australia and is currently available for streaming on Stan.

According to the official synopsis on IMDb, the film follows a retired former special force operative who tries to keep his son from being taken away by navigating the criminal world.

Luke Buckmaster from Guardian mentioned the film has a mechanical plot as if it was written by Artificial Intelligence. The article adds the death scenes have no meaningful emotional attachment and are merely represented as the “end of the prologue.”

Furthermore, the film is broken into blocks, the first block represents Ryan’s former life as an operative. The second one covers all the bonding between the father and the son while the third block is actually where the major plot of the film happens.

Transfusion is written and directed by Matt Nable. The film is produced by 16 people including Samantha Allwinton, Christelle Conan, and Christelle Conan among others. 

Transfusion 2023 Cast

Transfusion 2023 cast is star-studded with Sam Worthington, Phoebe Tonkin, Edward Carmody, and Matt Nable portraying the principal characters.

Sam and Phoebe play spouses in the film.

Sam Worthington Plays Ryan Logan

Ryan Logan is the principal character in Transfusion 2023. Logan is a retired special force operative who has served in Iraq. He is trying his best to keep his son from being taken by the authorities. To do so, Ryan is forced to join a boss in the criminal world.

Sam Worthington plays the lead character Ryan in the film. Sam is a seasoned English-born Australian actor. He is best known for his appearance in Avatar, Terminator, Hacksaw Ridge, and Everest, among others.

The Titan star made his acting debut with the 2000 television series JAG and has since collected 64 acting credits on his IMDb profile. Ten of his acting credits are for his upcoming roles, including Avatar 3, 4, and 5, scheduled to release in 2024, 2026, and 2028 respectively. 

The actor was born to his parents, Jeanne J and Ronald W. Worthington. The Call of Duty star is married to model  Lara Bingle Worthington, and the couple shares three children. They share sons Rocket Zot, Racer, and River together.

Phoebe Tonkin Plays Justine

Justine is the wife of the lead character Ryan. Justine is the mother of Ryan’s son Billy and is pregnant with his second child. The subplot of the family of three hanging out together after some emotional backstory is used to showcase the soft side of Ryan.

Phoebe Tonkin plays Justine in the film Transfusion. Tonkin is an Australian actress best known for appearances in The Vampire Diaries. She has appeared in other projects including, Tomorrow, When the War Began, H2O: Just Add Water, and The Originals.

The actress made her acting debut with the 2006 television series H2O: Just Add Water, where she portrayed Cleo Sertori for 78 episodes between 2006 and 2010. Since her debut, Tonkin has amassed 25 acting credits on her IMDb profile.

The Home and Away actress is 33 years old. Phoebe was born to her parents, Jannyfer Tonkin and Nicholas Tonkin. She boasts over 8.4 million followers on her Instagram account under her username @phoebejtonkin.

Edward Carmody Plays Billy Logan

Billy Logan is the son of Ryan Logan in Transfusion. Billy is a key character in the plot of the film as his carelessness and tendency to get into trouble forces his father Ryan to get involved with the underworld criminals. Billy is portrayed by Gilbert Bradman as an eight-year-old and later by Edwards when he grows up. 

Edward Carmody is a fresh actor. In fact, Transfusion is the actor’s debut film according to his IMDb profile. His Instagram account under username @edwardfridaycarmody has just shy of 1k followers at the time of writing this article. However, the number of his followers is expected to rise rapidly as his debut film Transfusion is doing very well in Australia.

On the other hand, 8-year-old Billy’s actor Gilbert Bradman has a spec more experience when it comes to acting compared to Edward. Bradman made his debut in 2019 with the television series Alphabet Street. He has since amassed 4 acting credits on his IMDb profile.

Matt Nable Plays Johnny

Johnny is Ryan’s first contact with the underworld. Johnny is a former SAS colleague who comes with an offer of a simple but illegal gig for Ryan to earn a few extra bucks. I do not recall when was the last time the easy illegal job was actually easy in a movie.

Matt Nable who portrays Johnny is actually the director and writer of the film as well. The former Rugby League player made his acting debut in the 2007 film The Final Winter. Since his debut, Matt has acquired 39 acting credits on his IMDb profile.

Some of his best motion pictures as an actor include Blue Murder: Killer Cop, Last King of the Cross, Hyde and Seek, and Arrow, among others. His writing credits include Transfusion, Last King of the Cross, Outlaws, and The Final Winter.

Nable whose full name is Matthew Nable was born to his father Dave Nable. His sibling Adam Nable is also a former Rugby League player. Matt also has started his own family with his wife Cassandra Nable and the couple shares one son Jesse Nable in their 23 years of marriage.

Where Was Transfusion Filmed?

Transfusion was filmed in Sydney and surrounding locations in Australia. The car crash scene of the movie was shot in Little Bay southeast of Sydney.

Sam was seen at Little Bay while filming the car crash scene during the lockdown, according to Daily Mail.

The actor wore brown boots, blue jeans, and bomber jackets flaunted his long hair that sat on his shoulder, and was seen clutching a script between takes at the filming location.

Detail information about the movie’s filming location is not available in the public domain.

However, Worthington was lucky enough to split his time between the Avatar franchise and Transfusion during the shooting, as both films were filmed in Sydney, New South Wales, Australia.

Where To Watch Transfusion 2023? Release Date and Trailer

Transfusion 2023 is available for streaming on the Australian streaming service Stan.

The film was made as Stan Original and released in Australia on January 20, 2023, directly on the streaming service. According to the film’s official streaming website, Transfusion is ready to download as well.

It features all the modern film features including 4k resolution, Dolby Atoms sound mixing, closed captions, and audio description, among others.

Transfusion’s trailer is available to watch on Youtube and IMDb page. The trailer takes the viewers through the whole journey of the film, starting from Logan’s struggles in Iraq to his trying to fit in as a normal person in society. 

Furthermore, the trailer also documents Ryan’s bond and disagreement with his son Billy. It also shows Ryan being soft with his pregnant wife Justine.

Overall, the trailer pretty much covers the idea for the film while enticing the audience to watch the full movie.
