Isabella Todaro Obituary: Hackettstown Mourns the Passing of Beatiful Soul

Publish date: 2024-01-20

This is very sad news to share with you that Isabella M. Todaro, 17, of Hackettstown, NJ, died on October 7, 2023, in Washington Township, Morris County. In this post, you will get all the information related to her death.

What is Isabella Todaro’s Cause of Death?

Todaro was killed in a crash with only one other car on Flocktown Road near Nestlingwood Drive in Washington Township, Morris County, at 2:25 a.m. The vehicle hit a utility pole and did a lot of harm. She was pronounced dead at the scene. Police said:

“The investigation is ongoing and no additional details will be released. We extend our deepest condolences to the driver’s family.”

Isabella wasn’t just like another Hackettstown High School student; she was a beloved part of our community, known for her bright smile, kindness and lively spirit. We are all still shocked by her quick death and are trying to come to terms with this terrible loss.

Where Will Isabella Todaro’s Obituary Ceremony be Held?

Wednesday, Oct. 11, from 2:00 p.m. to 6:00 p.m., friends and family of Isabella M. Todaro, 17, can visit at Trinity United Methodist Church, 213 Main Street, Hackettstown. After the viewing at 6:00 p.m., there will be a memorial service in the church to honor Isabella’s life.

A GoFundMe page was started for her family’s welfare. She was born in Livingston, NJ, on November 5, 2005, to Lisa Todaro and Louis Todaro. Isabella was in her last year at Hackettstown High School.

She worked at Heath Village and Stella G’s in Hackettstown. She loved her family and friends the most. We will miss her a lot. Lisa Ann Todaro and Louis Todaro, her sister Ashley Lipinski and her husband Christopher, her twin brother Louis E. Todaro and her nephew Owen Lipinski will miss her very much.

As we remember Isabella, we also send our condolences and wishes to her family, especially her twin brother Louis, who is a well-known football player at school. You can also share your condolences towards her family in the comments section below.
