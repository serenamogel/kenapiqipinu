EJ Johnson Children: Does EJ Johnson Have Children?

The 31-year-old socialite is not yet a father. As of January 2024, EJ Johnson does not have any children, either biological or adopted children.

He’s best known for participating in the E! reality television series; “Rich Kids of Beverly Hills” in 2014 as a recurring cast member in the first season.

Following his performance, he became a main cast member for the next three seasons. The show was canceled after four seasons.

Johnson then starred in his spin-off reality series EJNYC, one of the first reality series to profile a young LGBTQ person of color.

In 2014, he joined E! News as a news personality and commentator, covering shows for New York Fashion Week. He had a recurring commentary role on Fashion Police.

Later that same year (2014), Johnson also appeared as a guest commentator on Hello Ross. He was a guest star during season 4 of Real Husbands of Hollywood.

In 2016, he was featured in an It Got Better short documentary. In 2017, Johnson moderated a panel at BeautyCon called The Gender Revolution and was also a guest host on The Real.
