➡ Anzela Abbasi Biography

An emerging face of the Pakistani television industry, Anzela Abbasi is the daughter of the famed Pakistani actor, director, screenwriter Shamoon Ali Abbasi, and actress Javeria Abbasi. Anzela Abbasi has stepped into the field of showbiz and has appeared in many serials. She is popularly known for appearing in the serial “Baby” which was aired in 2017. Subsequently, she then appeared in many serials including “Mein Haar Nahin Manungi” aired in 2018.
