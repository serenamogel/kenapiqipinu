Jesse Watters | Career

Jesse began working as a production assistant at the Fox News Channel as soon as he finished college.

He joined the “The O’Reilly Factor” production team in 2003, and the following year he started starring in his own sections of the show “Watters’ World.”

Watters made his daytime talk show debut with “Outnumbered” in June 2014.

Later, in November 2015, Jesse launched “Watters’ World,” his own show on Fox News. The program debuted in early 2017 and now airs every Saturday at 8 pm ET.

Following that year, Watters joined the roundtable chat show “The Five” as a co-host.

Five commentators who analyze current events, pop culture, and various political topics are featured on the one-hour program during the weekdays.

Greg Gutfeld and Dana Perino, as well as a changing cast of other guest co-hosts, appear on the show with Jesse.
