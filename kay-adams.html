Who is Kay Adams?

The Television Personality Kay Adams is mostly known as Sportscaster. She works as the host of Good Morning Football on NFL Network.

With her amazing knowledge of football and beautiful appearance, she is seen on NBC Sports Network’s Fantasy Football Live. Similarly, she is also working for the DirecTV Fantasy Zone channel as a host during the football season.

Kay Adams – Professional Career

Adams’s career started as an in-game host for St. Louis Cardinals after gaining experience she entered the world of professional football.

She was in the fantasy football shows like SiriusXM Fantasy Drive and SiriusXM’s Livin’ the Fantasy. Even today, she is working as a co-host in DirecTV Fantasy Zone.

In 2012, on October 17 she appeared as a guest on the Late Late Show with Craig Ferguson. After being part of NFL Network’s Good Morning Football since 2016, she gained popularity giving her opportunities to work on special events as a host for the professional American football team Chicago Bears.
