Britt Nilsson's Bachelorette Suitor Brady Toops Keeps Posting PDA Pics

Publish date: 2024-05-27

Brady and Britt kissing in a tree! Bachelorette suitor Brady Toops, who left Kaitlyn Bristowe's season 11 for runner-up Britt Nilsson, has been continuously posting kissing photos ever since their relationship went public this week.

After the big reveal on episode 3 of The Bachelorette, both Brady and Britt posted PDA snaps of their full-fledged romance this week on Instagram. "Behind the scenes from Episode 3 of The Bachelorette," Brady wrote on Tuesday, along a black and white selfie with his current girlfriend. "The beauty herself, miss @brittkarolina y'all!"

On Wednesday, May 28, he shared even more kissing photos with the bright-eyed Bachelorette star. "Sorry mom, I couldn't help it," he joked via Instagram, alongside an image of himself planting a sweet smooch on Britt's smiling cheek.

Brady then followed up with another photo of Britt returning the favor. "She makes every picture better.." he wrote alongside the pic.
