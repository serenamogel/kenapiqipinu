Dylan Sprouse is an American actor who has an estimated net worth of US$8 million as of June 2023. He started off as a child actor and has since gone on to make a name for himself in the American television series industry.

Sprouse is also a business oriented person thereby making such an amount of money at just age 30. He has also signed a number of endorsement deals that all combine to make up his total net worth.

Who is Dylan Sprouse?

Dylan Thomas Sprouse, son of Matthew Sprouse and Melanie Wright was born on August 4, 1992 in Arezzo, Tuscany, Italy. He is a twin and his brother’s name is Cole Sprouse. There was 15 minutes between Dylan who came first and his twin.

Dylan was named after Welsh poet and writer, Dylan Thomas. At four months old, his parents relocated to Long Beach, California, their hometown.

Sprouse began his career on television alongside his twin brother in 1993. During which he shared the role of Patrick Kelly in Grace Under Fire until 1998. Couple years after, he made appearances in several films and television series with his brother.

Sprouse came into the limelight after starring alongside his twin again in Disney Channel series The Suite Life of Zack & Cody from 2005 to 2008.

Sprouse attended New York University’s Gallatin School of Individualized Study and obtained a four-year degree in video game design after The Suite Life on Deck ended in 2011.

In 2017, he was cast in the lead role of Lucas Ward for the thriller film Dismissed. In the same year, he appeared in a short film, Carte Blanche, and was cast in comedy film Banana Split as Nick.

Sprouse is married to his long term girlfriend and model Barbara Palvin. The loved birds have been together since 2018. Prior to moving to Los Angeles in 2021, they were residing in Brooklyn. They got married in July 15, 2023.
